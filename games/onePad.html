One-Time Pad Cipher

How to Play

Objective: Decrypt the given encrypted messages using the provided keys.

Gameplay:

  • Each round presents you with a question to decrypt a word or answer.
  • You will receive a randomly generated key for each question.
  • Type your answer in the input field and click "Submit Answer."

Controls:

  • Submit Answer: Click the "Submit Answer" button after typing your guess.
  • Replay: Click the "Replay" button to start a new game after finishing.
  • Show History: Click the "Show History" button to view your previous scores.
  • Clear History: Click the "Clear History" button to remove your score records.

Scoring:

  • Correct answers earn you points.
  • Incorrect answers will reveal the correct answer.

End of Game:

  • Your final score will be displayed after all questions are answered.
  • You can save your result and view your game history.

Tips:

  • Pay attention to the keys and hints provided for each question.
  • Practice makes perfect; try different words to improve your skills!
